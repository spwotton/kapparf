### Core questions
1. What public records show Italian government-linked entities doing business in Costa Rica from 2020 to present?
2. What exactly is the reported **$20M cadastral / mapping contract**: award date, contracting authority, scope, deliverables, subcontractors, data-hosting terms, imagery source, and retention rules?
3. Are there public statements, filings, annual reports, procurement documents, or press releases tying Costa Rican geospatial projects to:
   - Telespazio Argentina
   - e-GEOS
   - COSMO-SkyMed / CSG imagery
   - cloud processing platforms such as CLEOS
4. What is the most defensible description of the relationship between FinFisher / Gamma legacy entities and Italy?
   - direct corporate continuity?
   - reseller / talent / investor overlap?
   - parallel but separate ecosystems?
   - no evidence of a formal tie?
5. What Italian public documents mention exports, services, or dual-use authorizations relevant to Central America or Costa Rica?
6. What public evidence exists for LeoLabs infrastructure in Costa Rica, including location, service scope, partnerships, and regulatory footprint?
7. What U.S. public documents describe SDA / PWSA / Blackjack activity that could matter for regional context, without speculating beyond the source record?
8. Around **2025-01-14**, what actually happened in public records?
   - launches
   - press releases
   - corporate announcements
   - procurement milestones
   - regulatory filings
   - satellite service ramp-up notices
   - public tenders or amendments
9. Are there public FOIA logs, court records, export-control cases, parliamentary questions, NGO reports, or transparency requests involving these entities and Latin America?
10. Which claims circulating online are unsupported, exaggerated, or contradicted by primary sources?

### Required research standards
- Prioritize **primary sources** over commentary.
- Every key claim must have a citation.
- Separate **verified fact**, **plausible inference**, and **unsupported claim**.
- Do **not** infer covert targeting, mind-control, or hidden operations unless a source explicitly supports the claim.
- When evidence is absent, say **no public evidence located**.
- Flag entity-name confusion, shell-company claims, and rumor cascades.

### Required source types
Prioritize in this order:
1. procurement databases
2. corporate annual reports / investor filings
3. government gazettes and ministry reports
4. satellite operator press releases and technical docs
5. export-control reports
6. court filings and NGO litigation records
7. parliamentary questions / hearings
8. academic papers / trade publications
9. reputable investigative journalism
10. social posts only if they lead to primary records
