## 🔬 TECHNOLOGY OVERVIEW

**Core Concept:**
Standard audio processing (hearing aids, phones) cuts off frequencies above **8 kHz**. This patent describes a method to use **Extended High Frequencies (EHF)** (8 kHz - 20 kHz) to extract speech signals from noisy environments.

**Key Mechanisms:**
1.  **Fricative Energy:** Consonants like "s", "sh", "f", "ph" have significant energy **> 8,000 Hz**.
2.  **Directionality:** High frequencies are more directional than low frequencies. The ear (or sensor) can determine if speech is "directed at" the target based on EHF content.
3.  **Noise Immunity:** Background noise (traffic, hum) typically dominates low frequencies. EHF remains "stable and unmasked" in noisy settings.
